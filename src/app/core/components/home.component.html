Zero Trust Maturity Model Assessment Tool

This tool helps you assess your organization's maturity against the Zero Trust Maturity Model (ZTMM) framework. Define and manage your pillars, functions/capabilities, and technologies/processes, then conduct assessments and view results.

Configuration Assessment Results
Your Data Stays Private

All your assessment data is stored locally in your browser using IndexedDB. No data is sent to external servers - everything remains on your device for complete privacy and security.

Getting Started

Follow these steps to set up the tool manually:

1. Configuration

Start by setting up your Zero Trust framework. Define pillars, functions/capabilities, maturity stages, and technologies/processes that align with your organization.

2. Assessment

Conduct maturity assessments by evaluating your current state against each technology/process. Track progress with visual indicators.

3. Results

View comprehensive results and analytics. Export reports to PDF and track your Zero Trust maturity progression over time.
